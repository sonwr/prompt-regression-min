# Reviewer queue priority-rank scope checklist

Use this checklist before posting a reviewer handoff based on the exposed `priority_rank` winner.

## Checklist

1. Confirm the winning queue is named explicitly.
2. Confirm the runner-up is still visible when the margin is narrow.
3. Confirm the handoff sentence names the next action, not just the ranking.
4. Confirm the wording stays shard- or queue-scoped instead of sounding like a release-wide conclusion.
5. Confirm the supporting evidence matches the summary fields you actually exposed.

## Ready-to-post cue

If all five checks pass, the queue handoff is usually safe to post as a compact human-facing update.
