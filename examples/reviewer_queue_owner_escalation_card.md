# Reviewer queue owner escalation card

Use this short card when the next focus group is clear but no owner is assigned yet.

## Inputs to surface

- `next_focus_group`
- `next_focus_label`
- `next_focus_cases`
- `next_focus_share`
- `priority_rank`

## Recommended handoff sentence

`Escalate owner assignment for <next_focus_label>: it leads the queue with <next_focus_cases> cases (<next_focus_share>) and currently has no clear owner.`

## Why it helps

This keeps reviewer queue summaries actionable even when the queue ranking is already known.
