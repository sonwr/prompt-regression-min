# reviewer_queue_scope_handoff_card

Use this note when the next reviewer queue is clear but the handoff should stay intentionally narrow.

## One-line pattern

`Next reviewer focus: <queue label> first; keep the handoff scoped to <queue share> queue share / <source-case rate> source-case rate until the runner-up is rechecked.`

## Quick reminders

- Keep the current winner visible.
- Keep the runner-up visible if the lead is narrow.
- Mention source-case impact so reviewers understand the dataset footprint, not just queue order.
