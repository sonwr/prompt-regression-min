# Reviewer queue top-two checklist

Use this when the dominant reviewer queue and the runner-up are close enough that a one-line handoff could overstate certainty.

## Quick checks

1. Compare queue share before naming a winner.
2. Compare source-case rate so the visible lead is not just a small-sample artifact.
3. Re-check the exposed priority rank and tie mode.
4. Keep the runner-up visible when the lead is narrow.

## Ready-to-post sentence frame

`<winner>` stays the next reviewer focus, but `<runner_up>` remains close enough that the handoff should keep the runner-up visible.
