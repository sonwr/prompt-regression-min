# Reviewer queue priority-rank quick audit

Use this note when the summary already exposes `priority_rank` and you want a last fast check before posting a human handoff.

## Quick audit

- Is the selected queue still the highest-priority queue after tie-break rules?
- Does the handoff sentence mention the queue label, rank, and why it beats the runner-up?
- If queue share is small, does the note still explain why the rank matters more than raw count?

## Copy-ready sentence

```text
Next reviewer focus: <label> (priority rank <n>) because it outranks <runner-up> after the configured tie-break, even when raw queue counts are close.
```

## When to avoid it

Do not post a rank-only handoff when the queue metadata shows a tie that still needs a human decision.
