# Reviewer queue priority-rank owner-ready check

Use this note after the priority-rank winner is already exposed and you need one last owner-facing review before posting the handoff.

## 30-second audit

1. Confirm the winner queue still has the highest exposed priority rank.
2. Keep the runner-up visible when the lead is narrow.
3. Mention queue share or source-case impact if that is what made the winner credible.
4. End with the concrete next reviewer action.

## Copy-ready owner handoff

`Priority-rank winner: <queue>; keep <runner-up> visible if the lead is narrow, cite the deciding share/source-case cue, and assign the next reviewer action explicitly.`

## Hold instead of post when

- the winner changed after filtering,
- the rank is tied without a posted tie mode,
- the handoff sentence hides the runner-up despite a narrow lead,
- or the next reviewer action is still vague.
