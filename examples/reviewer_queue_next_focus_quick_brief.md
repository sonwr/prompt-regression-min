# reviewer queue next-focus quick brief

Use this when `next_focus_group` is already available and you need the shortest reviewer-ready status line.

## Three checks before posting

1. Confirm the chosen queue key and `priority_label` agree.
2. Confirm the queue still has the highest queued follow-up share after tie-breaks.
3. Confirm the advantage summary explains *why now* in one sentence.

## Copy-ready pattern

```text
Next focus: <priority label> / <queue key> — <queue share> queued follow-up, <active-case rate> active, <source-case rate> source; <advantage summary>.
```
