# Reviewer queue next-focus hold note

Use this when the exposed next-focus winner exists, but the lead is too narrow to sound decisive.

Status line template:

`Next reviewer focus stays on <winner>, but the lead is narrow enough that <runner-up> should remain visible in the same handoff.`

Quick checks:

- keep the winner label and priority rank visible
- mention the runner-up when tie mode is `tied` or the gap feels marginal
- do not describe the queue as settled if the rerun order could reasonably change on the next shard
