# Reviewer Queue Bundle Replay Note

Use this when a reviewer needs to reopen the exact queue decision together with its saved summary artifacts.

Recommended replay order:

1. Reopen the summary JSON to confirm the selected queue, pass rate, and source-case rate.
2. Reopen the markdown or HTML handoff that explains why this queue is next.
3. Post a short reviewer note only after the queue lead and artifact bundle still match.

Keep the sentence short: queue winner, why it leads, and which bundle to reopen first.
