# Reviewer queue next-focus decider

Use this note when the summary already exposes `next_focus_group`, but you still need to decide whether the lead is strong enough to post as the reviewer handoff.

## Fast check

1. Confirm the exposed next-focus queue has the highest priority rank.
2. Confirm the queue-share lead is visible, not tied.
3. Confirm the runner-up is still named when the lead is narrow.
4. Post a short handoff only when the winner, margin, and runner-up stay readable in one pass.

## Decision outcomes

- **Post now** — the next-focus queue is the unique leader and the margin is easy to explain.
- **Post with qualifier** — the next-focus queue still leads, but the runner-up is close enough to mention.
- **Hold for review** — the rank or queue-share lead is tied, unclear, or missing.

## Copy-ready lines

- Post now: `Next reviewer focus: policy, with the clearest queue-share lead in this run.`
- Post with qualifier: `Next reviewer focus: policy, but the safety queue remains a close runner-up.`
- Hold for review: `Do not post a next-focus winner yet; review the tied or ambiguous queue metadata first.`
