# Reviewer queue share + bundle owner status line

Use this when one rerun lane is already selected and you need a short owner-facing handoff.

Keep four details together:
- the dominant reviewer queue
- its queue share
- the saved JSON/Markdown/HTML report bundle path
- the owner or next reviewer

Template:

`Next reviewer focus: <queue label> first (<queue share> queue share); reopen <report bundle path>; owner: <owner>.`
