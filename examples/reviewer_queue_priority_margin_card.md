# Reviewer queue priority margin card

Use this note when the next reviewer focus is technically decided, but the lead feels small enough that a human reader may still doubt it.

## What to say

- Name the selected queue.
- State its queue share.
- State the runner-up queue share.
- Say the margin in plain language.
- End with the next action.

## Template

`Next reviewer focus: <queue> (<share>% of open review work), ahead of <runner_up> by <margin> points; review this queue first and keep the runner-up as the follow-up lane.`

## When to use it

Use this card when the JSON summary already exposes a next-focus queue, but the PR comment still needs a confidence sentence for humans.

## Why it helps

A tiny numeric lead is easy to overlook in raw JSON.
This card turns that lead into a deterministic handoff sentence without adding new scoring rules.
