# Reviewer queue next-focus rate card

Use this card when the reviewer queue already exposes a `next_focus` group and you need a short human-readable handoff.

## Minimum readout

Call out four numbers together:

- next-focus case count
- queue share
- active-case rate
- source-case rate

Example:

```text
Next focus: fix_regressions
- 4 case(s)
- 57.14% of queued follow-up
- 44.44% of active cases
- 33.33% of source cases
```

## Why this helps

- **Queue share** explains follow-up pressure.
- **Active-case rate** explains how dominant the group is among currently evaluated cases.
- **Source-case rate** keeps large filtered runs honest.
- **Case count** preserves concrete reviewer scope.

## Suggested handoff sentence

```text
Start with the next-focus group when it leads on queue share and still represents a meaningful share of both active and source cases.
```
