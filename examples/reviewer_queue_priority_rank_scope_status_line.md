# Reviewer queue priority-rank scope status line

Use this when the priority-rank winner is known and you need a single sentence that keeps the handoff scoped to the selected queue.

## Template

`Priority-rank winner: <queue> (rank <n>), but keep the handoff queue-scoped until <runner-up or missing proof> is resolved.`

## Examples

- `Priority-rank winner: policy-review (rank 1), but keep the handoff queue-scoped until the style-review runner-up gap is rechecked.`
- `Priority-rank winner: safety-audit (rank 1), but keep the handoff queue-scoped until the missing source-case note is added.`
- `Priority-rank winner: billing-edge-cases (rank 2), but keep the handoff queue-scoped until the narrow share lead is reconfirmed.`

## Post-before-you-post check

Make sure the line still shows:

1. the winning queue,
2. the visible rank,
3. and the reason you are not broadening the handoff beyond that queue.
